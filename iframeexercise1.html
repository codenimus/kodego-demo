The Cassava Maruya Recipe

Here is another recipe that anyone can enjoy for breakfasts or snacks, the Cassava or Kamoteng Kahoy Maruya. One of the many Filipino foodstuffs that are easy to prepare with simple ingredients. Share and savour with the family, or sell it for extra income.

Ingredients:

  • 2kg grated cassava, aka Kamoteng Kahoy
  • 1cup sugar - as for this recipe, we used the Segunda sugar (for those who do not know, it is like a mixture of brown and white sugar combined
  • 1/2 cup of evaporated milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • Margarine for cooking the cassava Maruya recipe
