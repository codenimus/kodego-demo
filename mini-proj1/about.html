OUR MISSION

To proclaim, teach and defend
the holiness of God in all its fullness
to as many people as possible.

WHAT WE DO

Providing ways for Christians to read, study, and converge to become rooted around God's Truth.

We Publish

God reavealed Himself in a book (Heb. 1:1). In that sense, reading is an essential part of every Christian's life. For now, we publish online articles with downloadable PDFs so that Christians can grow in their understanding of God and of themselves. We plan to publish physical books as well.

We Broadcast

Faith comes by hearing (Rom. 10:17). That makes hearing the word of Christ is essential to our spiritual life. Every week, we air podcasts, video teaching series and more. We're always adding trustworthy and curated teachings from other reformed teachers will soon be available 24/7.

We Converge

Iron sharpens iron (Prov. 27:17). Aside from converging online through livestreams, we aim to expand our Christian gatherings at physical events to study God's Word for mutual edification; to bless the local church and strengthen its members for greater service.
